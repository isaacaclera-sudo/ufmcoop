Invitation for Membership

Good day to everyone.


Thank you for giving us the opportunity to share about UFM Coop and invite you to become a member to us!

Let me begin with a simple question.
Have you ever wondered what would happen if ordinary Filipinos decided to work together—not just as individuals trying to survive—but as a community cooperating to create opportunities for each other?

For many of us, life has always been about hard work. We work for our families. We work for our children. We work for a better future.

But sometimes, despite all our efforts, we feel that progress is slow because we are trying to move forward alone.
That is why cooperation matters. When people cooperate, something powerful happens.
Resources are shared. Opportunities expand. And communities become stronger.
This is the vision behind the United Filipino Movement for National Transformation Consumers Cooperative, or UFM Cooperative.
UFM Cooperative was created to bring people together—teachers, workers, entrepreneurs, farmers, professionals, overseas Filipinos, community leaders—so that we can work together to create sustainable opportunities. Through cooperation, we can develop initiatives such as:

• agro-forestry livelihood programs
• sustainable agriculture projects
• cooperative business hubs
• community enterprises
• environmental restoration programs
• and many other opportunities that benefit both people and communities.

But more than the programs, what truly matters is the spirit behind them. UFM Cooperative believes that when people cooperate with integrity, discipline, and shared purpose, we can build communities that are not only economically productive but also socially responsible and environmentally sustainable. Our goal is not simply to build projects. Our goal is to build a movement of cooperation.
A movement where people help each other grow. A movement where communities become stronger together. A movement where Filipinos participate in shaping a better future.
This cooperative belongs to its members. And every member becomes part of something larger than themselves. So today, I invite you to consider joining UFM Cooperative. Not just as members, but as partners in a shared vision. A vision where cooperation creates opportunity. A vision where communities thrive.
And a vision where Filipinos work together to build a future that benefits not only our generation—but the generations that will come after us. Thank you very much, and I hope you will join us in this cooperative journey.
