Overview

UFM Cooperative aims to empower communities and drive sustainable, inclusive growth by promoting cooperative enterprise, innovation, and shared responsibility both locally and globally.

Vision

To become a globally connected Filipino cooperative institution that empowers communities, advances sustainable enterprise, and inspires a culture of shared prosperity through cooperation, innovation, and responsible stewardship of resources.

UFM Cooperative envisions a future where Filipino communities, both in the Philippines and across the world, participate actively in building resilient local economies, protecting the environment, and creating opportunities for inclusive growth.

Mission

UFM Cooperative exists to mobilize the power of cooperative enterprise to uplift communities, develop sustainable livelihoods, and strengthen national development through collaborative partnerships.

We pursue this mission by:

  • Promoting

    cooperative entrepreneurship and inclusive economic participation.

  • Developing

    sustainable agriculture, agro-forestry, and community enterprises.

  • Supporting

    innovation, digital platforms, and cooperative hubs for market access.

  • Building

    partnerships with educational institutions, civic organizations, businesses, and global Filipino communities.

  • Advancing

    • social responsibility, environmental stewardship, and community resilience.

Core Values

Cooperation

We believe that shared effort and collective ownership create stronger communities and more sustainable prosperity.

Integrity

We uphold transparency, accountability, and ethical governance in all cooperative activities.

Sustainability

We promote responsible stewardship of land, resources, and the environment for future generations.

Innovation

We embrace new ideas, technologies, and cooperative models that improve lives and expand opportunities.

Empowerment

We invest in people by expanding access to livelihood, education, and opportunity.

Community

We nurture partnerships that strengthen social bonds and advance the common good.
