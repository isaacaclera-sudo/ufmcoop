About Us > Key Inititatives

Strategic Development Platform

UFM Cooperative operates as an integrated cooperative development platform, enabling members and partners to participate in initiatives that combine sustainable enterprise, environmental restoration, and community development.

Sustainable Agro-Forestry Development

Community-based agro-forestry programs that promote environmental restoration, responsible land use, and long-term rural livelihood opportunities.

Cooperative Hub Network

A decentralized network of community-based cooperative hubs that bring together members from education, business, faith-based organizations, civic groups, and local communities.

Farm Tourism and Eco-Development

Development of sustainable tourism and environmental learning destinations such as the Hiraya Cooperative Agro-Forest Farm and Eco-Resort in Real, Quezon.

Digital Cooperative Platforms

Technology-driven cooperative solutions that connect producers, consumers, and entrepreneurs within a collaborative marketplace.

Community Enterprise and Livelihood Programs

Support for small-scale entrepreneurs, farmers, and rural communities through cooperative-based enterprise development.
