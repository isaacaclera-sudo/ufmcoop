Advocacy

Global Filipino Participation

UFM Cooperative provides an opportunity for Filipinos around the world to engage in cooperative initiatives that contribute to sustainable development in the Philippines.

Through responsible cooperative participation, overseas Filipinos can support projects that strengthen rural economies, promote environmental sustainability, and expand community livelihood opportunities.

Partnership Ecosystem

UFM Cooperative works with a diverse network of partners including:

• Educational institutions and academic networks

• Civic organizations and service clubs

• Faith-based organizations

• Small and medium enterprises

• Local government units

• Filipino diaspora communities

• International development partners

These collaborations help expand the reach and impact of cooperative initiatives across sectors and regions.

Commitment to Sustainable Development

UFM Cooperative supports initiatives aligned with global priorities for sustainable development.

These include environmental restoration and responsible land stewardship.

Inclusive economic participation and rural livelihood development are also key focuses.

Community resilience and local capacity building strengthen long-term impact.

Sustainable agriculture and agro-forestry further support these goals.

Through cooperative innovation and collaborative partnerships, UFM Cooperative seeks to contribute to a more sustainable and inclusive future for Filipino communities.
